How to Play

Movement

  • Arrow Keys or hjkl - Move character
  • Numpad 1-9 - Move in 8 directions

Items

  • g - Pick up items
  • i - Open inventory
  • x - Examine items
  • u - Use consumables

Equipment

  • w - Wield weapons
  • W - Wear armor
  • r - Remove equipment

System

  • s - View skills
  • a - View achievements
  • q or Escape - Quit
  • Enter - Confirm selections
